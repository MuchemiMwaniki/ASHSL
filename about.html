About AutoServe Holdings Ltd

Welcome to AutoServe Holdings Ltd, your premier partner for high-quality auto parts and accessories in Kenya. Established with a vision to provide reliable and affordable automotive solutions, we pride ourselves on a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower vehicle owners and workshops with easy access to genuine and certified aftermarket auto parts, ensuring optimal vehicle performance, safety, and longevity. We strive to be the most trusted and efficient auto parts supplier in the region.

Why Choose Us?

  • Extensive Inventory: We stock a vast range of parts for various makes and models, from common consumables to specialized components.
  • Quality Assurance: All our products undergo stringent quality checks to meet international standards. We partner only with reputable manufacturers.
  • Competitive Pricing: We leverage our strong supplier relationships to offer the best prices without compromising on quality.
  • Expert Team: Our knowledgeable staff are always ready to assist you in finding the right part for your vehicle.
  • Fast & Reliable Delivery: We understand the urgency of repairs, offering prompt delivery services across Kenya.

Our Values

Integrity, Customer Focus, Quality, and Innovation are the pillars of our business. We believe in building long-term relationships based on trust and mutual respect.
