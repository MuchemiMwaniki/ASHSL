About ASHSL

ASHSL (Auto Spare Hub Solutions Ltd) is your premier partner in Kenya for high-quality and reliable auto spare parts. With years of experience in the automotive industry, we are dedicated to providing an extensive range of parts for various vehicle makes and models, ensuring your vehicle performs at its best.

Our Mission

To be the leading provider of auto spare parts in Kenya, recognized for our commitment to quality, affordability, and exceptional customer service. We aim to empower vehicle owners and mechanics with easy access to genuine and reliable components.

What We Offer

  • Extensive Inventory: A vast selection of spare parts, from engine components to body parts and electrical systems.
  • Quality Assurance: We source parts from reputable manufacturers and suppliers, ensuring genuine and durable products.
  • Competitive Pricing: We strive to offer the best prices in the market without compromising on quality.
  • Expert Advice: Our knowledgeable team is always ready to assist you in finding the right part for your specific needs.
  • Efficient Delivery: Fast and reliable delivery services across Kenya to get you back on the road sooner.

Brands We Work With

We supply parts for a wide array of international vehicle brands, ensuring comprehensive coverage for our customers.

Our Commitment to You

At ASHSL, we are committed to building long-term relationships with our customers based on trust, reliability, and unparalleled service. We understand the importance of quality parts for the safety and performance of your vehicle, and we strive to exceed your expectations with every transaction.

Thank you for choosing ASHSL as your trusted auto spare parts provider.
