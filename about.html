About AutoServe Holdings Ltd

Your trusted partner for quality auto parts and exceptional service in Kenya.

Our Vision

To be the leading and most reliable supplier of automotive spare parts and accessories in Kenya and the broader East African region, known for quality, integrity, and exceptional customer service.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, genuine, and affordable automotive spare parts to our customers, ensuring their vehicles run smoothly and safely. We achieve this through efficient sourcing, robust logistics, and a commitment to customer satisfaction.

Who We Are

AutoServe Holdings Ltd is a dynamic and rapidly growing automotive spare parts supplier based in Nairobi, Kenya. With years of experience in the automotive industry, we understand the critical need for reliable, durable, and cost-effective parts. Our extensive network of reputable manufacturers and distributors, both local and international, allows us to offer a diverse inventory that caters to a wide range of vehicle makes and models.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Quality Assurance: We stock only genuine OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and high-quality aftermarket parts that meet international standards. Each part undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure performance and longevity.
  • Extensive Inventory: From engine components and braking systems to suspension parts, electricals, and bodywork, our comprehensive catalogue ensures you find exactly what you need.
  • Competitive Pricing: We believe that quality should not come at an exorbitant price. Our efficient supply chain management allows us to offer competitive prices without compromising on quality.
  • Expert Team: Our knowledgeable and friendly staff are always ready to assist you in identifying the correct parts for your vehicle, offering expert advice and personalized service.
  • Timely Delivery: We understand the urgency of vehicle repairs. Our robust logistics network ensures swift and reliable delivery of parts right to your location within Nairobi and surrounding areas.

Our Commitment

At AutoServe Holdings Ltd, we are committed to building long-term relationships with our customers based on trust, transparency, and mutual respect. Whether you are a garage owner, a mechanic, or an individual car owner, we strive to be your first choice for all your automotive spare parts needs.
