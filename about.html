About ASHSL

Welcome to **ASHSL**, your premier destination for high-quality automotive spare parts. Located in Nairobi, Kenya, we pride ourselves on being a reliable source for both **new and used spare parts**, catering to a wide range of vehicle makes and models.

Our mission is to ensure your vehicle runs smoothly and safely by providing genuine and reliable parts at competitive prices. We understand the importance of quality and efficiency in getting your vehicle back on the road, which is why we meticulously source our inventory to meet diverse customer needs.

What We Offer:

  • New Spare Parts: Directly sourced from reputable manufacturers and distributors, ensuring authenticity and durability.
  • Used Spare Parts: Carefully inspected and tested pre-owned parts that offer a cost-effective solution without compromising on quality.

Brands We Work With:

We supply parts for a comprehensive list of international and local vehicle brands, including but not limited to:

Toyota Nissan Mitsubishi Subaru Honda Mazda Mercedes-Benz BMW Audi Volkswagen Ford Chevrolet Hyundai Kia Isuzu Hino

Whether you're looking for parts for a saloon car, SUV, commercial vehicle, or truck, ASHSL is your one-stop shop.

Our commitment extends beyond just sales; we aim to build lasting relationships with our customers through transparent service and expert advice. Our knowledgeable team is always ready to assist you in finding the exact part you need.
